Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Data We Collect

Spendly collects the following information to provide and improve our service:

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password. This information is used to authenticate your account and send service-related communications.

Expense Data: All expense records you log, including amounts, categories, dates, descriptions, and attachments. This data is stored on our secure servers and belongs entirely to you.

Usage Information: We collect non-identifying information about how you interact with Spendly, such as pages visited, features used, and time spent. This helps us improve the app and understand user behavior patterns.

Device and Technical Information: We automatically collect information about your device, browser type, IP address, and operating system to ensure compatibility and security.

How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

Provide Services: Authenticate your account, store your expenses, and deliver the core functionality of Spendly.

Improve Our Service: Analyze usage patterns and feedback to enhance features, fix bugs, and optimize performance.

Security: Protect against fraud, detect vulnerabilities, and ensure the integrity of our platform.

Communication: Send you transactional emails (password resets, account confirmations) and optional notifications about new features or important updates.

Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and court orders when required.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols. All expense data is stored on secure servers with appropriate access controls and backups. We implement multiple security layers to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We take the security of your data seriously and continuously update our security practices to meet industry standards.

Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or share your personal expense data with third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may use third-party service providers for:

Infrastructure and Hosting: Cloud services to store and manage your data securely.

Analytics: Non-identifying usage analytics to understand and improve our service.

Payment Processing: If applicable, payment providers may process transactions on your behalf.

These service providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are only authorized to use your data as necessary to provide services to Spendly.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

Access Your Data: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

Correct or Update: Update or correct any inaccurate information in your account.

Delete Your Account: Request deletion of your account and associated data. We will remove your information from active systems within 30 days, though backups may be retained for a limited period.

Opt-Out of Communications: Unsubscribe from optional newsletters and marketing communications at any time.

Children's Privacy

Spendly is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy here and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of Spendly after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, concerns about how your data is handled, or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us through the support channels available on Spendly.
